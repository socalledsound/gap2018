GAME #2

For this project I want you to create an alternate reality game.

Or, at least, an alternate reality.

I'd prefer that it be a Utopian reality, that's just me, I'm an optimist at heart.

In other words, design your ideal place/space/world....and engage us, pull us into it, lure us along into your little fantasy.

As ever, the "game" aspect of this can be as involving (game-like) as you like.

In other words, if it wants to be more story, or film, or painting or installation than it is "game", then make it so!

That said....typically, 'alternate realities' share a few salient characteristics.

1. mixed, multiple media. a singular phenomenon or perspective which extends itelsf across media helps to reinforce the idea of an alternate reality and creates resonances which are rich and unpredictable.

2.manifestations in the 'real' world of specific idiosyncratic symbols, objects, characters which originated in the texts or digital media of an alternate reality.

3.Mysteries or puzzles which lead us into the "rabbit hole".

4."This is not a game". Do not re-assure your audience that you are in fact not crazy, that they will be ok, etc.
